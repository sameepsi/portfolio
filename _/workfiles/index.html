Frequently Asked Questions

What is an online Flex Loan
• A TQC online Flex Loan is a line of credit that gives you convenient access to the cash you need, right when you need it. Once you are approved, you can get cash advances for any amount, as low as $10.00, or up to what is available on your total credit limit. Cash advances will be deposited directly into your checking account.
• When cash is advanced, the payback on the advance(s) is also very flexible; you may pay it all back at once, make minimum payments which pays the balance down more gradually, or pay MORE than the minimum payment amount so that the principal balance is reduced more quickly.

When will I have access to the money that I borrow online?

• Any Flex Loan approved, or cash advance made, online prior to 4:00pm CST will be made available by 4:00pm CST the next business day in your bank account. If cash is needed faster than that, please call (615) 717-9800 and we can quickly get you pre-approved and have the cash waiting in a store for you to come and pick up at your convenience.

What do I need to get a Flex Loan online?

• State or Federally issued ID.
• Open and active checking account with online banking and consistent deposits.

What are my payment options?

• Typically, customers pay bi-weekly, monthly, or semi-monthly according to their pay cycle.
• A statement will be sent to your email, 14 days prior to any due date, which will show finance charges, the minimum payment due, current balance, etc.
• You may pay only the minimum payments due, which include finance charges and a 10% per month, or 5% bi-weekly, principal reduction amount that is based upon the principal balance as of the date of the last statement issued.
• You may pay the loan off at any time. The finance charges are calculated based on the average daily principal balance. . Tennessee Quick Cash encourages our customers to always pay more than the minimum payment.

How much does it cost?

• You pay for the days that you have the money that you borrowed. You will pay a periodic interest rate of 24% per annum plus a customary fee at a rate of .7% per day on the average daily principal balance. The combined annualized rate, or APR, for this loan is 279.50%, however this loan is meant to be used as a short-term loan. For instance, if you borrow $500.00 for 7 days, then the finance charges you will pay when you pay it off on the 7th day will be $26.80, therefore the total payoff that day would be $526.80. Will I have online access to my Flex Loan?

• Yes, there will be an online portal email sent to you the night that you open your Flex Loan that will give you your user name and initial password for online access to your account.
• You can make payments, receive cash advances, view account details and change your reoccurring payment type right from the comfort of your own home.

Are there any penalties or late fees associated with this loan?

• There is no penalty for paying the Flex Loan off, nor are there any annual fees or late fees. You only pay for the days that you borrow the money. The loan can be paid off at any time with no pre-payment penalty.

How much can I borrow on a Flex Loan?

• A flex loan can be opened for as little as $50.00, or as much as $4000.00. Your credit limit is determined by your monthly income. Once opened, a cash advance can be made for as low as $10.00, up to what is available on your line of credit.
